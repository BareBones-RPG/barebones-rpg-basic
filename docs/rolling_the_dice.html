Barebones RPG Basic Mechanic

Each Player Character (PC) has a set of attributes, each represented by a number from 1 to 5. To perform an action tied to an attribute, the player must roll a number equal to or lower than the attribute's value using a six-sided die (d6) to be a successful test. If the number is higher, this is a failed test.

Critical v Fumble

Whenever a player/NPC/Monster rolls a 1 on the six-sided die. This is a critical success!

    Combat Critical Success
  • Melee or Missile Attack: Player automatically does strike damage.
  • Defense: Player avoids attack and gets a free attack (player rolls damage without rolling to hit).

Whenever a player/NPC/Monster rolls a 6 on the six-sided die. This is a fumble (failure).

    Combat Fumble
  • Melee or Missile Attack: Weapon breaks
  • Defense: Player Character takes double damage and reduces armor by one tier (d6-->d4-->d2-->0)
