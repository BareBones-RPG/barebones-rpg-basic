## WHAT DO YOU NEED TO PLAY THIS GAME?

* Pencils
* Index cards or character sheets
* A single six-sided die (**d6**)
* 2 to 6 players (1 Game Master/Referee)
* An encounter map or virtual tabletop (VTT)
* Tokens or miniatures
* Plenty of imagination

---

## HOW TO PLAY

Each Player Character (PC) has attributes (1 to 5). To perform an action, roll **equal to or lower** than the attribute's value on **1d6**.

* **Example:** A PC with **Might 3** must roll a 3 or less to force open a door.

---

## CHARACTER CREATION

BareBones RPG is system-agnostic and setting-neutral. The Referee (Ref) helps players generate stats using one of two methods:

1. **Rolling:** Roll **3d3** or **4d3**. The total is a pool of points to distribute.
2. **Flat Array (Optional):** Assign **8 points** (Gritty) or **10 points** (Heroic).

**Limits:**

* Every attribute starts with 1 point.
* No attribute can be 0.
* No attribute can exceed 5.

---

## LEARNING THE ATTRIBUTES

* **AGILITY:** Defend, balance, swim, flee.
* **INSIGHT:** Perceive, aim, charm, recall knowledge.
* **MIGHT:** Crush, lift, hit, grapple, endure.
* **WILLPOWER:** Resist fear, use relics, cast spells, perform rituals.
* **LUCK:** Your edge when fate fails.

---

## USING LUCK

Spend **1 Luck** to:

* Gain a **+1 or +2 Boost** to a test (before rolling, max 5-in-6).
* Act first in initiative for the round.
* Neutralize a fumble (bend fate).
* Attempt a **Feat of Fate** (Ref’s discretion).

**Regaining Luck:** Regain 1 point (max 5) when you roll a **6** on any test or through cinematic roleplay awarded by the Ref.

---

## REFEREE’S CORNER

The Referee also has **LUC** points to:

* Decrease PC Willpower resistance.
* Deal double damage.
* Neutralize a PC’s Critical success.

---

## HELP & HINDER

The Referee determines modifiers based on the narrative before a roll.

* **Boost (+1):** Helping another PC. Max +2 Boost.
* **Burden (-1):** Hindering conditions (prone, bad tools). Max -2 Burdens.
* *Note: Chances cannot go higher than 5-in-6 or lower than 1-in-6.*

---

## MOVEMENT

* **Close:** 5 feet
* **Near:** Up to 30 feet
* **Far:** Within sight
* *Moving through foes requires an **AGI** test.*

## PRAYER & SPELLCASTING

### Divine Penance

If a Divine PC fumbles a prayer, they lose 1 WIL and must perform:

* **Act of Atonement:** 1 full day of service.
* **Sacrificial Offering:** 5–10 gold or a valuable item.
* **The Vow:** A temporary self-imposed Burden (-1 to tests).

### Casting Mishaps (d6)

| Roll | Mishap | Consequence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Delay | Effect occurs next round on a random target. |
| 2 | Strain | Burden (-1) on all tests for next round. |
| 3 | Blast | Deals 2 Hits to those near caster. |
| 4 | Presence | Distraction causes party-wide Burden (-1) to WIL. |
| 5 | Stunned | Thrown 1d6 ft; Burden (-1) to AGI/MIG next round. |
| 6 | Upper Hand | Foe gets a free Critical Attack; Armor reduced 1 tier. |

## COMBAT PHASE

1. **Initiative:** Roll 1d6 per round. **1-3:** Players first. **4-6:** Enemies first.
2. **Player Turn:** Attack, Cast, Move, or Use Gear.
3. **Defense:** Players roll to defend against enemy attacks.
* **Dodge:** Roll vs **AGI**.
* **Parry:** Roll vs **MIGHT** (Reduces weapon tier instead of Hits).



### Damage & Hits

* **0 Hits:** PCs don't die immediately. Instead, attributes decrease:
* **Might 0:** Dead.
* **Agility 0:** Dazed (No movement).
* **Insight 0:** Disheartened (Must be snapped out of it).
* **Willpower 0:** Catatonic (Requires 3 days rest + WIL test).
