Welcome to BareBones RPG

A rules-light tabletop RPG using a single d6. Cinematic. Fast. Flexible. Flavorful.

This reference document is an adaptation of the freely available BareBones RPG: Basic Editions SRD presented here for the first time in glorious HTML and contains everything needed to play a complete role-playing game capable of handling anything from single sessions to decades-long campaigns.
